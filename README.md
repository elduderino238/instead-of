## Instead of Adobe Suite
### Photoshop > GIMP
> GNU Image Manipulation Program, commonly known as GIMP, is a free and open-source raster graphics editor used for image manipulation and image editing,

<img width="800px" src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/1d/GIMP_2.10.jpg">

### Illustrator > Inkscape/Krita
> Inkscape is a free and open-source vector graphics editor for GNU/Linux, Windows and macOS.

<img width="800px" src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/47/Inkscape_1.3_screenshot.png">

> Krita is a free and open-source raster graphics editor designed primarily for digital art and 2D animation. 

<img width="800px" src="https://krita.org/images/pages/application-screenshot.webp">

### After Effects > Blender
> Blender is a free and open-source 3D computer graphics software used for creating animated films, visual effects, art, 3D-printed models, motion graphics & more.

<img width="800px" src="https://www.blender.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/07/blender_render-1280x720.jpg?x12104">

### Premier Pro > KdenLive/Shotcut
> Kdenlive is a free and open-source video editing software based on the MLT Framework, KDE and Qt.

<img width="800px" src="https://kdenlive.org/wp-content/uploads/2022/01/271174170_1089343691850009_6872343577648842521_n.png">

> Shotcut is a free and open-source, cross-platform video, audio, and image editing program for Linux, macOS and Windows.

<img width="800px" src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5c/Shotcut_Video_Editor_Screenshot.jpg">

### Lightroom > Rawtherapee/Darktable
> RawTherapee is application software for processing photographs in raw image formats, as created by many digital cameras.

<img width="800px" src="https://www.rawtherapee.com/rt57_field_sunset.jpg">
